CRR – OWN FUNDS
SECTION 1 – COMMON EQUITY TIER 1 CAPITAL

Article 26 – Common Equity Tier 1 capital items

1. Common Equity Tier 1 capital shall consist of the following items:
(a) instruments that meet the conditions set out in Article 28 or, where applicable, Article 29;
(b) share premium accounts related to the instruments referred to in point (a);
(c) retained earnings;
(d) accumulated other comprehensive income;
(e) other reserves.

2. Institutions shall calculate Common Equity Tier 1 capital as the sum of the items listed in paragraph 1, after applying the regulatory adjustments specified in Articles 32 to 36.

Article 28 – Conditions for Common Equity Tier 1 instruments

1. Capital instruments shall qualify as Common Equity Tier 1 instruments only if all of the following conditions are met:
(a) the instruments are issued directly by the institution;
(b) the instruments are fully paid up;
(c) the instruments are classified as equity under the applicable accounting framework;
(d) the instruments are perpetual;
(e) distributions are paid out of distributable items.

Article 36 – Regulatory adjustments to Common Equity Tier 1 capital

1. Institutions shall deduct the following from Common Equity Tier 1 items:
(a) losses for the current financial year;
(b) intangible assets;
(c) deferred tax assets that rely on future profitability.

For the purposes of this prototype, regulatory deductions are assumed to be zero unless explicitly stated in the reporting scenario.
