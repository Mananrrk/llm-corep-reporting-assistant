COREP – OWN FUNDS (TEMPLATE C 01.00)

Template C 01.00 shall be used to report the composition of own funds.

Column 010 – Amount
Institutions shall report the amount of each own funds component.

Row 010 – Common Equity Tier 1 capital
This row shall include the total Common Equity Tier 1 capital after regulatory adjustments.

Row 020 – Common shares
Institutions shall report the amount of common shares that qualify as Common Equity Tier 1 instruments in accordance with Article 26(1)(a) and Article 28 of the CRR.

Row 030 – Retained earnings
Institutions shall report retained earnings as defined in Article 26(1)(c) of the CRR.
Interim profits shall only be included where they have been verified by the institution’s external auditors.

Row 040 – Accumulated other comprehensive income
Institutions shall report accumulated other comprehensive income in accordance with the applicable accounting framework.

Row 050 – Other reserves
Institutions shall report other reserves that qualify as Common Equity Tier 1 items under Article 26(1)(e).

Row 060 – Total Common Equity Tier 1 capital
This row shall equal the sum of rows 020 to 050.
